# How to Add Ads to Your App and Make Money (Step-by-Step Guide)

This guide explains how to register, integrate, and earn money from ads in your mobile app using **Google AdMob**, the most beginner-friendly ad network.

---

## Step 1: Identify Your App Type

Before starting, confirm your app platform:

- Android app
- iOS app
- Web app
- Game app (Unity, Flutter, React Native, etc.)

> Ad integration steps depend on the platform and framework.

---

## Step 2: Choose an Ad Network

### Recommended for Beginners: Google AdMob

**Why AdMob?**
- Works on Android and iOS
- Easy setup
- Trusted and widely used
- Good monetization performance

Other options (advanced):
- Meta Audience Network
- Unity Ads (games)
- AppLovin
- ironSource

---

## Step 3: Create a Google AdMob Account

1. Visit the Google AdMob website
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click **Get Started**
4. Fill in:
   - Country
   - Time zone
   - Currency
5. Accept the terms and conditions

Your AdMob account is now active.

---

## Step 4: Register Your App in AdMob

1. Go to **AdMob Dashboard**
2. Click **Apps**
3. Select **Add App**
4. Choose whether the app is published or not
5. Select platform:
   - Android or iOS
6. Enter:
   - App name
   - Package name (Android) or Bundle ID (iOS)

This generates your **App ID** (required later).

---

## Step 5: Create Ad Units

Navigate to:
**AdMob → Your App → Ad Units → Create Ad Unit**

### Common Ad Types

- **Banner Ads**
  - Small ads at top or bottom
  - Easy to implement
  - Beginner-friendly

- **Interstitial Ads**
  - Full-screen ads
  - Shown between screens

- **Rewarded Ads**
  - Users watch ads for rewards
  - Best for games

> Recommended to start with **Banner** and **Interstitial** ads.

Each ad unit provides an **Ad Unit ID**.

---

## Step 6: Add AdMob SDK to Your App

### General Steps
- Add AdMob SDK dependency
- Add App ID to app configuration
- Initialize AdMob when the app starts

> Implementation depends on platform (Android, iOS, Flutter, Unity, etc.).

---

## Step 7: Use Test Ads (Very Important)

⚠️ **Never click real ads while testing**

- Use Google-provided **Test Ad IDs**
- Prevents account suspension
- Ensures safe development

---

## Step 8: Publish or Update Your App

- Upload your app to:
  - Google Play Store
  - Apple App Store
- Wait for store review approval

Real ads will show only after:
- App is live
- AdMob approves the app

---

## Step 9: Set Up Payments

1. Go to **AdMob → Payments → Settings**
2. Add:
   - Personal or business details
   - Tax information
   - Bank account
3. Minimum payout threshold: **$100**
4. Payments are issued monthly

---

## Step 10: Optimize Ad Revenue

After ads start showing:

- Place ads at natural breaks
- Avoid excessive ads
- Use rewarded ads if possible
- Monitor AdMob analytics
- Enable mediation for higher earnings

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Clicking your own ads
- Using real ads during testing
- Showing too many ads
- Generating fake or bot traffic
